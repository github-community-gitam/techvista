Videos promise to take you on a journey, display your creative side, share a thought, and give you a new perspective on the world.

What if we gave you a chance to do all at once?

Gather your best Lens and the smartest parts of your business brains as GitHub brings to you it's next event in EPOCH 1.0!

Only three things you have to do:

  1. Make a cinematic product video (like this)
  2. Make a sales pitch video (how would YOU sell the product you chose?)
  3. Submit!

  • The team is recommended to have at least one videographer, one editor, one producer, and one marketing strategist.

  • The team is also required to make a sales pitch for the same product.
  • One finalist team from each campus will be selected based on the demonstration of the product features and uses, videography, creativity, editing and how good they pitch the product.
  • Plagiarism at any stage of the competition will lead to disqualification.
  • The final round will require all the teams to present a product video, UIUX mock-up, pitch the same product in-person in GITAM Bengaluru.
